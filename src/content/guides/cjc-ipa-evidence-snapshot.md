CJC-1295 and ipamorelin get sold as a combo story online. Treat them as **separate research threads** that people mash together — not one magic molecule with one evidence grade.

## What’s actually going on

- Each component has its own literature, half-life chatter, and claim set
- Blend ratios on research vials vary by supplier — identity and concentration still need docs
- “GH optimization” marketing often outruns what any single paper supports

## Awareness framing

Mixed evidence means some human or clinical-adjacent signals may exist for pieces of the story, while popular gym outcomes remain noisy. Sleep, training, calories, and other drugs confound almost every forum log.

If a post won’t separate CJC from ipamorelin (or won’t show a lot COA), you’re reading a brand pitch.
