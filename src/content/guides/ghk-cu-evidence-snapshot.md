GHK-Cu (copper peptide) has a real footprint in **cosmetic / skin science** discussions. That is not the same lane as injectable research-vial culture that borrows the name.

## Split the routes

- Topical cosmetic literature ≠ research peptide vial claims
- Identity, concentration, and copper chemistry matter; “copper peptide” on a label is not a full story
- Injectable talk online often imports cosmetic papers as if they were systemic protocols — they aren’t

## Awareness framing

Mixed evidence can be honest: some skin-related research exists, while systemic / recovery memes need a different evidence bar. Always ask **route**, **formulation**, and **what outcome was actually measured**.
