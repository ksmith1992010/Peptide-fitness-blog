Not every peptide paper is about a drug-like ligand. Desk pulls also include **mechanical β-hairpin switches** and work on **synthetic nanoassemblies** perturbing intracellular homeostasis.

## The literacy move

Ask: is this paper optimizing a **material / tool**, a **probe**, or a **therapeutic candidate**? Most of these titles are tools. Tool papers get mis-captioned online as “new peptide breakthrough for humans.”

## Practical filter

| Signal | Usually means |
|--------|----------------|
| Mechanical / materials properties | Not a wellness product |
| Nanoassembly + cell homeostasis | Model biology; high misuse risk in blogs |
| No clinical endpoint | Do not invent one |
