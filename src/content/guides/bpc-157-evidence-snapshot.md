BPC-157 is one of the most meme-famous “healing peptides.” Popularity ≠ proof.

## What shows up in research discussions

- Preclinical and animal work exploring tissue and gut-related pathways
- Mechanistic hypotheses that sound compelling in slides
- Very limited high-quality randomized human clinical evidence for the claims flooding social media

## Awareness framing

If your only sources are before/after posts, you’re looking at **anecdotes**. Anecdotes can inspire questions. They can’t carry the claim.

Regulatory and clinical conversations around research peptides continue to evolve — treat online protocols as internet content, not care plans.
